TINDOG TAGUM

Help a little, give a lot

Tindog Tagum is a non stock non profit youth organization founded by progressive youth leaders in the city of Tagum with a common vision for youth and community building.

A youth organization with a vision of creating networks, youth community engagement, and social development, Tindog Tagum prides itself as a grassroot youth organization dedicated in advancing pro-people agendas. Efforts were them made to legitimize the organization's existence through applying for registration at the Securities and Exchange Commission and was granted approval on July 15, 2022.

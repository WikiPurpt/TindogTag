HOW TO GET INVOLVED

Become a volunteer or help with our fund for future projects.

It all starts with your decision to act rather than turn away. You fulfill a need, and that causes a domino effect. Families are able to escape poverty, start working toward a brighter future, and develop a receptive outlook toward new life. You have the ability to change a life, a family, or a whole community with one action.

Donate or sponsor an event

We take pride in urging all Filipinos to take part in the volunteerism of the Tindog Tagum by becoming a donor for present and future projects. We guarantee our sponsor to be given acknowledgement and be updated concerning the projects we are planning from time to time.

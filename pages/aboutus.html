WHAT ARE WE

About Tindog Tagum

Tindog Tagum is a non stock non profit youth organization founded by progressive youth leaders in the city of Tagum with a common vision for youth and community building.

Tindog Tagum was officially registered last July 14, 2022, organized geared towards the participation of youth volunteers in the nation-building and community development. It aims further to favor the marginalized sector through its flagship programs that creates capacity-building activities such as relief activities, leadership trainings, youth development, livelihood programs, environmental works and other programs that will provide better opportunities to the community.

A youth organization with a vision of creating networks, youth community engagement, and social development, Tindog Tagum prides itself as a grassroot youth organization dedicated in advancing pro-people agendas. Efforts were them made to legitimize the organization's existence through applying for registration at the Securities and Exchange Commission and was granted approval on July 15, 2022.

Call for youth members followed on July 27, 2022 and on August 6, 2022 first ever members met up for the first time in its acceptance and induction rites.

OUR VISION


Tindog Tagum Youth Organization Inc., envisions a community of youth volunteers inspired and equipped to realize a future sustainable for the coming generations.

OUR MISSION


• To provide opportunities and capacity-building activities such as relief programs, environmental works, and other programs that will provide better opportunities to the community.

• To provide human development programs to help the marginalized sector a level of sustainability and empowerment, and adopt measures to promote a spirit of generosity and selfless giving among members, individuals, and institutions that will help develop programs directly involved in nation-building and community development, and
